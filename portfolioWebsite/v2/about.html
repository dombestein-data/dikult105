About Dombestein Data

Dombestein Data is a small web development studio based in Bergen, Norway.

I build fast, clean websites and systems with a focus on readability, maintainability, and doing things properly from the start.

No bloated setups. No mystery plugins. No "We'll fix it later" solutions that quietly fall apart after launch.

Most things are possible. The question is how clean you want it.

If you need something delivered in a rush, rest assured it will never be a sloppy result. If you want something that is built to last, that's where I thrive and do my best work.

The guy behind it all

I'm Isak - a Digital Culture student at the University of Bergen, developer by habit, and the person behind Dombestein Data.

I care a lot about structure, clarity, and systems that make sense - both in code and in collaboration. I enjoy starting from a blank slate and building things up deliberately, rather than stacking plugins and hoping for the best.

I tend to focus on the small details that make a site feel solid: performance, accessibility, predictable behavior, and setups that are understandable six months down the line, not just on launch day.

Tech Stack

I work with modern tools, like Astro, React, PostgreSQL and Cloudflare - not because they're trendy, but because they let me build fast, predictable systems that are easy to maintain.

The exact stack matters less than the outcome: clear structure, sensible defaults, and solutions that don't require constant babysitting.

  • Astro, React, TailwindCSS, shadcn/ui
  • Hono, PostgreSQL, Drizzle ORM
  • Cloudflare Workers, UploadThing, Axiom
